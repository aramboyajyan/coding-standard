Pay attention to the comments - they have to be at the beginning and end of elements with IDs.

If there are 2 or more elements with IDs used as wrappers, it is enough to add comments only to the parent.

Unique elements with their own HTML tags (usually footer and header) should also have comments at the beginning and at the end.

There is no need to give them special ID or class, unless required by the layout.
